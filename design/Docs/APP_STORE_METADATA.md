### Description

> Skinzone is your personal digital companion for tracking your wellness and beauty journey with the Skinzone clinic. Designed to replace physical loyalty cards and keep you connected, the app gives you instant access to your treatment history and rewards progress right from your phone.
>
> **Key Features:**
>
> **Digital Loyalty Tracker**
>
> - Say goodbye to lost paper cards or unexpected points expiration.
> - View your Total Points and see exactly how many points you earned from your last visit.
> - Track your progress toward a Free Visit or other rewards automatically.
>
> **My History**
>
> - Keep a complete log of your self-care journey.
> - Access a detailed list of your past Visitations and treatments, so you always know when your last appointment was and what services you received.
>
> **Profile Management**
>
> - Easily update your personal details and upload a profile picture to personalize your account.
> - Manage your password and security settings securely.
>
> **Stay Informed**
>
> - Receive notifications about your visits and account updates directly to your device.
>
> Download Skinzone today and keep your beauty routine in your pocket!
